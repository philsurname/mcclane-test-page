John McClane

Retired Detective with the New York Police Department

I was a detective with the NYPD from 1977 until 2013. I was instrumental in resolving the Nakatomi hostage crisis in Los Angeles in 1988 and was the lead officer combating terrorists during the Dulles International Airport incident of 1990. I was also the lead investigator during the New York City bombings in 1995. During my career I was commended and reprimanded by my superiors on several occasions. I eventually retired in 2013.

A series of Hollywood movies have been made about my policing career.
